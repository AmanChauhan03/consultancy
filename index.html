About Us

We constitute a dynamic team of India’s exceptionally innovative professionals. Our past work includes a robust track record of building highly successful organizations, driving large-scale campaigns and extensive initiatives in the remotest parts of India, and resolving formidable challenges and complex issues. Presently, we stand as one of the fastest-growing startups in India.

The team has evolved into a platform for young professionals aspiring to engage closely with elected representatives and decision-makers, actively participating in the governance of the country. We are driven by our core purpose to ignite impactful societal change by drawing attention towards issues and problems that concern them the most.

Indian elections are essentially a grassroots exercise and while we strive to modernize political campaigns, we recognise the strategic value of traditional methods. Our campaigns are a composite blend of both techniques thereby making them more people-centric. Each day, our team diligently crafts strategies for some of the most prominent figures in Indian politics. For us, every moment is ShowTime.
